Perfume will turn your life upside down

Christian Dior

French designer, founder of fashion house Cristian Dior. In 1942 Christian Dior create own perfumery company, then grown into a company Christian Dior Perfume.

“It is enough to open the vial, and all my dresses will appear, a each woman, which the I dress, leaves for a plume of desires. A perfume - is necessary addition personality woman, it is final chord for dress, it is rose, which Lancre signed their the painting.”

Return of fashion

In his first collection 1947, Dior create absolutely new collection - New Look. This was "romantic line", with new crinoline, thin waist and adjacent bodice. In that silhouette he embodied own represent about feminity, which was missing during the war with her clothes "labor service" for woman.

D&G

History brand

Dolce&Gabbana - Italian fashion house, founded by designer Domenicano Dolce. Until 2011, the fashion house represented also youth line D&G, which now joint with premium Dolce&Gabbana.

“ Each our creation contains particle us. Behind each model there is a story, an idea, a little mystery”

Founders ' biography

Domenico Dolce was born August 13, 1958 on the outskirts of the Sicilian city of Palermo in a poor family managing a small sewing enterprise.

In accordance with the traditions of Sicily boy by the age of six began to help parents in their business. For dexterity in work of small Domenico called "Mozart of sewing business".
